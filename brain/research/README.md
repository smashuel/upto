# Research

Competitor audits, UX research, spikes, experimental notes — everything that isn't a decision yet.

## How to use

- Drop competitor screenshots, design references, and note-taking here freely
- Frontmatter: `type: research`, `status: draft | in-progress | archived`
- When research leads to a decision, write an ADR in [decisions/](../decisions/) and link back
- When research leads to a plan, write a plan in [plans/](../plans/) and link back
